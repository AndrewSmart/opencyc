To change the #$Microtheory (Mt) that the displayed meta-assertions are asserted in, enter the new microtheory into the input window, then press the [Change Mt] button. The assertion governed by these meta-assertions is displayed, in its EL form, at the top of the page. The affected meta-assertions are displayed in their EL form at the bottom of the page, with checkboxes pre-selected.Un-checking any of the checkboxes will leave the Mt of the respective meta-assertions the same.

Changing the meta-assertion's Mt will delete the meta-assertion from the old microtheory and reassert it into the new one. To simply add another meta-assertion to a different microtheory, while still leaving the original meta-assertion intact the old Mt, go back to the Assertion Display Frame, click on "Assert Similar", and enter the original meta-assertion using a new Mt. For help with the Assert Similar Formula tool, see Assert Similar Formula Help.

To take advantage of constant completion, enter the first part of a microtheory name, then press the [Complete] button.

To clear the input field, press the [Clear] button.

To abort the edit completely and return to the previous page, press the [Abort Edit] button.
