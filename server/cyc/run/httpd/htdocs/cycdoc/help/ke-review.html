This page allows you to set up appropritae filters for review of constants and assertions that have been created. It's meant to make reviewing KE easier!

By turning on tags and links, relevant unreviewed constants and/or assertions will get a little tag which links to a review page. These pages allow the reviewer to assert that they approve of the constant or assertion as well as to attach additional comments to the constant or assertion.

Filters for these specific adornments can also be used, including the start and end date, and the particular cyclists who must be the #$myCreator for the assertion and or constant.

To restore the settings to the state they held when entering this page, press the [ Current Values] button.

To abort the edit completely and return to the previous page, press the [Back] or [Cancel] buttons.
